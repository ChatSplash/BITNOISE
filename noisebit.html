BITNOISE-8 V7.1 Guide

BITNOISE-8 is a pocket procedural synth lab. It generates endless cartridge beats, lets you perform with a stylus ribbon, and adds a freeform XY sound pad for live effects and experimental tones.

Main Controls

  • Start plays the current cartridge.
  • Generate creates a new motif-based loop.
  • Mutate changes the pattern without replacing everything.
  • REC writes ribbon notes into the melody lane while playing.
  • Clear empties the pattern.

Sequencer

  • Melody holds generated or recorded notes.
  • Bass adds low-end movement.
  • Kick, Snare, Hat form the drum machine lanes.
  • Tap any cell to edit it directly.

Piano Ribbon

  • Slide horizontally to change notes.
  • Higher touch = brighter sound.
  • Lower touch = heavier sound.
  • Black regions are sharp notes placed between the outlined white zones.
  • Hold latches notes; Glide bends between notes.

Freeform Pad

  • Lead sweeps a live synth voice.
  • Bass creates deep growls.
  • Noise creates static, scratch, and modem-like textures.
  • Drone makes held evolving tones.
  • X controls pitch, Y controls tone weight, and fast motion adds glitch.
  • Multiple fingers stay active independently.

Synth Lab

  • Chip is crunchy arcade.
  • FM is metallic and bell-like.
  • Wave is hollow and dreamy.
  • Hybrid blends engines per note.
  • Harmony adds fifths, octaves, chords, or ambient support tones.
  • Rain, Ocean, and Space create procedural background textures.

Library, Locks, Pads, Visuals

  • Save and reload cartridges in the local library.
  • Locks protect lanes during Generate and Mutate.
  • Performance pads trigger Fill, Drop, Glitch, and Bass hits.
  • The visualizer reacts to sound, touch, engine personality, and environment mode.
